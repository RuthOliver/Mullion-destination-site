The Chocolate Factory

The Chocolate Factory & Craft Centre is located on the road between Mullion Village and Mullion Cove. Over the last decade it has become well known for its unique range of attractions. The centre is open 350 days a year from 10 am to 5 pm with FREE parking for customers to the centre and FREE admission.
